Everyone knows a car with an efficient engine will not bring you to your destination without wheels and best tires. Not only best tire depends on the driving style of the driver but also on the speed and the condition you can drive at. How to choose the tires? The most noteworthy factor is the cost. Similarly, the more expensive the tire, the better it is.
Firstly, decide what you want from your tire. One can judge which tires are suitable based on a few points: • Size of a tire. A wide tire provides more grip than a narrow tire. • Load rating. A high profile tire is for a comfortable drive, whereas a low profile tire can be damaged easily. • The right tire tread depends on your need. More full treads perform well in the rain, and narrow treads perform less well in water. • Keep your tires inflated because it helps in providing more contact with the road. Furthermore, the driving conditions indicate what tire you want to put in your car.

Bullying

Bullying is repetitive behavior where kids and teens are very nasty, and they talk negatively. It can be physical, emotional, and cyberbullying. Those who are bullied are likely to be depressed, helpless, feel sad and alone, faces health issues, harmful eating patterns, and no interest in any activity. Similarly, those who bully are abusive, drug addict, fight more, and engage in wrongdoings. The most important question is how to deal with it? No matter what a bully says, you should not think and feel ashamed of who you're. Spend some time in doing positive things such as exercise, eating healthy food, reading books, etc. Nowadays, cyber-bullying is very common. It doesn't require any face to face contact, and it can happen anywhere, at any time. To deal with it, one needs to avoid communication from the cyberbully by unplugging from technology and meet new people or share feelings with your parents.
